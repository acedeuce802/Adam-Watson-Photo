About Me

From Metro Detroit, I bring an engineer's precision and a racer's perspective to action photography. My engineering background taught me to appreciate the technical side of cameras, while years of racing bikes—mountain, gravel, and road—gave me an insider's understanding of the moments worth capturing. Whether I'm toeing the start line or behind the lens, I'm there for the energy, the effort, and the story.
